Teamwork Test

Instructions

To build up your partnership's teamwork, you two will work together to play a game of brick breaker!

Controls:

The person on the left will control the blue paddle. The person on the right will control the red paddle. Move your paddle in game by moving left and right in real life!

Goal:

The two balls will bounce off of any surface (paddles, bricks, walls), but if you let them touch the ground, you'll lose!
If a ball collides with a brick, it will break it!
How to Win:If you destroy all bricks!
How to Lose:If both balls touch the ground!
